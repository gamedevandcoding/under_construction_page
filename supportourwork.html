What’s GameDev&Coding.?

It’s a project started by a self-taught developer named Marco, convinced that it's possible to make great videogames, devlogs, free tutorials, free educational content by putting the community and its feedback always at the center of an ecosystem, which aims to give what the fans and gamers want, need, dream the most.
GameDev&Coding aims to give to gamers great videogames really enjoyable to explore and play, without any mystery boxes, microtransactions, invasive advertisements.
Furthermore, It aims to help people learn about programming, and game development through online videos on social media, totally for free without any hidden or additional costs.
